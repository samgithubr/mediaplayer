TANDOORI BIRYANI

Ingridents

  1. 2kg-Rice
  2. 1kg-Chiken
  3. 2cup-Oil
  4. 5-Onions,Tomatos
  5. Gringer,Garlic,Chilly
  6. Garam-Masala
  7. 3L-Water
  8. 2kg-pot
  9. Green-Yellow Food Colors

Procedure


        To Make the Tandoori chicken biryani first we have to marinate the chicken 
 and have to fry it over a medium flame tandoor .
 so,now take a bowl to marinate our chicken so start the marination process.
 add chicken in bowl and then curd , garam masala ,chicken tandoor masala ,tarmaric
 Gringer Garlic paste and some amount of butter then and lime juice in it.
        
        mix this things together well and then fry them over a tandoor.
next we have to cooke our rice so for that take a pot and pour water into that        
some tej patta oil , elichi and boil the water for some time 
then add our rice into the water cooked the rice well

        then inside a pot make chicken sukka curry and over that add or boild well cooked rice.
add some colors our the rice to make it look good and then add or fry tandoor chicken.
keep it on a medium flame for while .

        after some time our biryani will e ready to serve .
